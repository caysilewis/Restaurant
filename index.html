What is 4 Corner's

Here at four corners we are dedicated to providing our guests with an authentic international experience. We serve food from four countries known for harboring cuisine rich in taste and culture. We are a very pretigious restaurant located in the Hell's Kitchen district of New York City. Our chefs are specially selected for the knowledge they possess about their country's cuisine. Since opening in 2003, 4 Corners, many celebrities have dined here including celebrity hacker Jonathan Cooper, singer Taylor Swift, famous chef Savannah Craft, actress Jennifer Anniston, former president Bush, and many more.

Founder

The founder of Four Corners is a young misses named Caysi. Having moved to New York City, she quickly acquired the funds to start a business through her graphic design company. Realizing that she not only had the ability to open her own resaurant but the appreciation of food, she decided that she could add to her legacy by opening 4 Corner's. She chose the most prestigious location of the Hell's Kitchen district of New York City.

What to expect

4 Corners is open nightly from 5pm until our last party leaves. We are open 365 days a year to icrease opportunities for our guests to dine with us. We do require a dress code as 4 Corners is an elegant and premeire dining experience. Formal attire or business casual is required to dine at 4 Corners. This means women must wear dresses, and men must wear a sports coat or a suit. Sandals, hats, and shorts are not permitted. Please plan to make reservations 6-12 months in advance as seating is highly competitive. If you do not follow our dresscode, regardless of if you have a reservation, you will not be seated.
